## Oral microbiome freezer stability code

This RMarkdown file and datasets represent the code and data used to produce the tables and figures for the "Long-term stability at -80°C of oral wash and saliva samples for microbiome analyses" manuscript under review at Microbiology Spectrum.

Healthy participants provided oral wash and saliva samples using Scope mouthwash and the OMNIgene ORAL collection device, respectively. DNA was extracted from an aliquot of each sample type at baseline, the V4 region of the 16S rRNA gene sequenced, and additional aliquots were then similarly extracted and sequenced after being stored for approximately one month, 12 months, and five years after collection. Intraclass correlation coefficients (ICC) and 95% confidence intervals (CI) were calculated for four alpha-diversity metrics, the first two principal coordinates of four beta-diversity matrices, and the 14 most abundant genera.
